Inputs

Inference Happy is only used to infer energy split by inversion. It does not directly define the baselines.

Baselines

Daily energy for each baseline is calculated as: (xans × 250) + refill (150 if checked) + natural regen. Active baselines are included in the results. Inactive baselines stay visible for quick toggling.
Results are based only on active baselines.

How to Use It
Copy two rows from Growth Details in TornStats, paste them into the quick paste box, then click Autofill Inputs. Your Frontier Score is a number representing how well you did against the strongest baseline you were compared against. It is approximately the percentage of gains you had, compared to the gains you would have had following a given baseline. Numbers over 1 are better than baseline (1.25 -> 125% of baseline), numbers below 1 are worse than the baseline (.5 -> 50% of baseline)
